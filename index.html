Flockr Privacy Policy

Welcome to Flockr, an application for creating, managing, and sharing events with friends and family. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: We collect your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Event Data: Information about events you create, including event name, date, time, location, and guest lists.
  • Device Information: We may collect data such as your device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers to ensure app stability and performance.
  • Usage Data: We track how you interact with the app to understand user behavior and improve our features. This may include pages visited and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the app and its services.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our app's functionality.
  • To communicate with you about your account, events, and important updates.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process.
  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can also request to have your account deleted at any time by contacting us.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@flockr.com.

  • Name

  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)

b. Event Information

  • Event details (e.g., name, date, time, description)
  • Invitees’ information (e.g., names, email addresses, or phone numbers)

c. Location Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Enable you to create and manage events.

Flockr will never sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
